1. Introduction

At Zak Kitchen, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our reservation system.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Reservation details (date, time, number of guests)

2.2. Technical Information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Session data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1. We use your personal information to:

  • Process and confirm your reservations
  • Send reservation confirmations and updates
  • Communicate with you about your reservation
  • Improve our services

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

4.2. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

4.3. We use Google OAuth for secure authentication.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1. We use Google services (Gmail API) for email communications.

5.2. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Cookies and Session Data

6.1. We use session cookies to maintain your login state.

6.2. These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.

7. Your Rights

7.1. You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Data Retention

8.1. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

8.2. Reservation data is typically retained for 12 months unless required by law to keep it longer.

9. Changes to This Policy

9.1. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

9.2. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

10. Contact Us
